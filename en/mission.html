Our Mission is

To foster gender relations based on equity, respect, and inclusion, creating safe and discrimination-free spaces. We work to eradicate violence and inequality, promoting active participation and empowerment of all people, regardless of their gender identity or expression.

Aspire to build a society where diversity is valued and where everyone can fully develop in an environment of respect and freedom.
