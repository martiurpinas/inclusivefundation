Welcome to InclusiveFundation

InclusiveFundation is a non-profit organization that promotes equality and respect in gender relations. Our team works to raise awareness, educate, and support people of all gender identities and expressions, striving for a fairer and more inclusive society for everyone.

Through workshops, campaigns, and activities, we aim to sensitize society about the importance of equity and respect, as well as provide resources and support to those who need it.
