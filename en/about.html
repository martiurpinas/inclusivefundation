About Us

InclusiveFundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting equality and respect in gender relations. Our goal is to raise awareness, educate, and support people of all gender identities and expressions, working for a more inclusive and equitable society.

InclusiveFundation has a multidisciplinary team committed to defending human rights and diversity, developing projects and activities that contribute to social transformation.
