What kind of activities do we do?

  • Workshops and educational talks on gender equality, diversity, and human rights.
  • Awareness campaigns in communities, schools, and public spaces.
  • Psychological and legal support for people affected by gender discrimination or violence.
  • Inclusive cultural and artistic events that promote participation and coexistence.
  • Collaboration with other organizations and institutions to strengthen the support network and resources.

All our activities are aimed at fostering respect, empathy, and inclusion in society.
